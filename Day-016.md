✅ Problems Solved

1. Sort the People

Platform: LeetCode (LC 2418)

Approach:

- Paired names with heights.
- Sorted the pairs in descending order of height and returned the names.

Time Complexity: O(n log n)

Space Complexity: O(n)

---

2. Minimum Number of Arrows to Burst Balloons

Platform: LeetCode (LC 452)

Approach:

- Sorted balloons by their ending coordinates.
- Used a greedy strategy to determine the minimum number of arrows required.

Time Complexity: O(n log n)

Space Complexity: O(1)

---

3. Merge Intervals

Platform: LeetCode (LC 56)

Approach:

- Sorted intervals by their start time.
- Merged overlapping intervals while traversing the list.

Time Complexity: O(n log n)

Space Complexity: O(n)

---

🧠 Key Learnings

- Sorting is often the first step in interval and greedy problems.
- Custom sorting simplifies many real-world interview questions.
- Greedy algorithms frequently rely on sorted input.
- Mastering sorting patterns improves performance in array-based problems.
