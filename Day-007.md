✅ Problems Solved

1. Container With Most Water

Platform: LeetCode

Approach:

- Used two pointers at both ends of the array.
- Moved the pointer with the smaller height to maximize the area.

Time Complexity: O(n)

Space Complexity: O(1)

---

2. 3Sum

Platform: LeetCode

Approach:

- Sorted the array.
- Fixed one element and used two pointers to find the remaining pair while skipping duplicates.

Time Complexity: O(n²)

Space Complexity: O(1)

---

3. Longest Repeating Character Replacement

Platform: LeetCode

Approach:

- Used a variable-size sliding window with a frequency map.
- Shrank the window whenever replacements exceeded "k".

Time Complexity: O(n)

Space Complexity: O(26)

---

4. Permutation in String

Platform: LeetCode

Approach:

- Maintained a fixed-size sliding window.
- Compared character frequencies to determine if a permutation existed.

Time Complexity: O(n)

Space Complexity: O(26)

---

🧠 Key Learnings

- Two Pointers are powerful for sorted arrays and optimization problems.
- Sliding Window reduces many O(n²) solutions to O(n).
- Frequency maps are commonly combined with sliding windows.
- Sorting + Two Pointers is a common interview pattern.
